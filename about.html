Museum Story

Founded in 1924, MuseumX began as a modest collection of regional artifacts and has grown into one of the world's premier cultural institutions.

What started as a single gallery dedicated to local heritage has evolved into a sprawling complex spanning 120,000 square feet. Today we welcome over two million visitors annually and partner with institutions across 40 countries.

◎

Mission

To inspire curiosity and foster understanding of human culture and natural history through exceptional collections, innovative exhibitions, and transformative educational programs that reach audiences of all ages and backgrounds.

◉

Vision

To be the world's most accessible and innovative museum — where technology and tradition converge to create meaningful connections between people, artifacts, and the stories that define our collective identity.
